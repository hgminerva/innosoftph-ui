About Us

Know more about us!






Innosoft Solutions Services Inc. is a software company that focuses on development and delivering business software such as Point-Of-Sales Systems (POS), Financial Management Information Systems (FMIS) and Human Resource Information System (HRIS) to Micro, Small and Medium Business Enterprises (MSMBE) within the Philippine Archipelago.

The Company was founded on April 24, 2008 by three friends namely Mr. Jason Yap, Engr. Harold Glenn Minerva and Mr. Jerico Canete. Mr. Yap was into computer hardware distribution and retailing while the other partners where focused into the software development.

Innosoft Solutions served more than three hundred (300) customers from various industries including trading, wholesale/retail, hospitality, healthcare, academic, government, logistics, manufacturing and non-government service organization.

It is currently a member of the PSIA (Philippine Software Industry Association).

  Who we are

Cebu Innosoft Solutions Services Inc. is the leading local support solutions provider of Information Technology (IT) Services to commercial and government IT Projects within Visayas and Mindanao Areas in the Philippine Archipelago.

Our target industries varies widely from wholesale and retail trading businesses, manufacturing and production, hotel and hospitality, healthcare, academic institutions, logistics, government / non-government organizations and service organizations.
