Point-Of-Sale (POS) System is a 3-in-1 software that caters to micro, small and medium sized businesses (MSMB)

Its module includes Sales, Accounts Receivable and Inventory. It is designed to be light and simplified to make users easily adapt and understand the flow of operations yet powerful enough to gather vital information for making business decisions.

By using POS, you can check your inventory in real-time and any given period. Sales and Collection Summaries can be generated instantly and accurately. With these reports you will be able to control the operations of your business, minimize inventory pilferage, and monitor you cash.
